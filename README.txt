# 🛒 Customer Sales Dashboard

This project presents an analytical view of customer purchase data including products, payment methods, geographies, and customer ratings. The dashboard supports business decisions related to product performance and regional sales.

## 📌 Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel
- Python
- SQL

## 📊 Key Insights
- Top Performing Product Categories and States
- Customer Ratings Distribution
- Sales Breakdown by Country, State, and Payment Method
- Quantity and Revenue Trends over Time
